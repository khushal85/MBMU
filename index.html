MBM UNIVERSITY

A State University established by the Government of Rajasthan in September, 2021 by upgrading the erstwhile M.B.M. Engineering College which is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India (established in August, 1951). The university boasts of its high academic & technical standards. MBMU is Considered as the pioneering technical institution of the Rajasthan State. It offers a gamut of courses both at PG and UG level. The university is committed to providing its students with an education that combines rigorous academic study and developing a far more ambitious, integrated and influential environment that will best serve our nation.
